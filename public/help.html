Voting User Guide

Step 1: Access the Platform

Open a web browser on your computer or mobile device.

Visit the E-vote platform's website (link to the platform).

Step 2: Log in to the Platform

Click on the "Login" button.

Enter your credentials.

After registration, your digital signature is automatically created. You can check this in your profile.

Step 3: Choose the Election

Once logged in, you will see a list of available elections in which you are eligible to participate.

Click on the election name you want to vote in (e.g., Presidential Election).

Review the candidates or options listed for the election.

Step 4: Cast Your Vote

Select your preferred candidate or option by clicking on the corresponding button.

Double-check your selection to make sure it reflects your choice.

Step 5: Track Your Vote

You have the option to track your vote in real-time using the tracking feature on the profile page.

Click on the "Track My Vote" option in the menu to see the status of your vote.

The tracking system ensures that your vote is securely recorded and remains confidential.

Step 6: Logout and Exit (Optional)

Once you have verified your vote and completed the process, click on the "Logout" button.

Ensure that you safely exit the platform to maintain your account's security.
